Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our crystal products, services, and ordering process. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us!

📦 Ordering & Pricing

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Standard MOQ is 50 pcs per design for most products. Custom orders typically have an MOQ of 100 pcs. Contact us for specific MOQ information on your desired products.
Do you offer free samples?
Yes! We provide free samples (up to 3 products). You only pay for shipping cost. Free shipping on sample orders when you place a bulk order over $500. Request samples here →
Can I get a price quote before ordering?
Absolutely! Contact us with your requirements (product type, quantity, specifications) and we'll provide a detailed quote within 24 hours. You can also download our catalog for price references.
Do you offer bulk discounts?
Yes! We offer tiered pricing for bulk orders. The larger your order quantity, the lower the price per unit. Contact us for custom quotes on large orders. Typical savings: 10-25% on bulk orders.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept T/T (bank transfer), PayPal, Western Union, and Alibaba Trade Assurance. For new customers, we typically require 30% deposit and 70% before shipment.

🚚 Shipping & Delivery

What is your lead time?
  • In-stock items: 3-5 business days
  • Custom orders: 15-20 business days
  • Expedited shipping: Available for urgent orders

Lead time may vary based on order quantity and complexity.

Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to 50+ countries worldwide. We work with reliable international shipping partners including DHL, FedEx, UPS, and sea freight for large orders. Typical shipping times: 5-15 business days depending on destination.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, we'll provide you with a tracking number. You can track your shipment directly through the carrier's website. We also provide updates throughout the shipping process via email.
What if my order arrives damaged?
We offer a quality guarantee on all products. If you receive defective or damaged items, we will replace them free of charge. Our products are inspected before shipment to ensure quality. Contact us immediately if you receive damaged items.

✨ Customization & Services

Do you offer OEM/ODM services?
Yes! We offer full OEM/ODM services with 15+ years of experience. We can customize:
  • Design and style
  • Size and dimensions
  • Color and finish
  • Logo engraving
  • Packaging

Free design consultation is included!

Can you add my logo to the products?
Yes! We offer logo engraving services on all our crystal products. We can engrave your company logo, brand name, or any custom text. This is perfect for promotional items, branded products, or corporate gifts.
What materials do you use?
We use high-quality K9 crystal for most products, known for its clarity and brilliance. We also use premium metal bases (gold-plated, chrome-plated, etc.) for candelabras and chandeliers. All materials are certified and meet international quality standards.
