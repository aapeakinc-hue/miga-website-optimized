About MIGAC Crystal Crafts

Your trusted partner for premium crystal candle holders and decorative crafts since 2014

Our Story

Founded in 2016, MIGAC Crystal Crafts Co., Ltd. has grown from a small family workshop into a leading manufacturer of premium crystal candle holders and decorative crafts. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the elegance and beauty of crystal craftsmanship to customers worldwide.

Located in Pujiang County, Zhejiang Province – known as China's "Crystal Capital" – we benefit from access to the finest raw materials and a skilled workforce with generations of crystal crafting expertise. This strategic location enables us to maintain exceptional quality while keeping costs competitive.

Over the past 10+ years, we've invested heavily in advanced production technology, quality control systems, and talent development. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of 182+ international clients across 50+ countries and regions.

Today, our 5,000 square meter factory produces over 5,000 different product styles, ranging from elegant candle holders to stunning decorative pieces. Each product reflects our dedication to craftsmanship, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
