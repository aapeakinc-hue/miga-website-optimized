Our Story

Founded in 2009 as Kampong Crystal Arts and Crafts Co., Ltd. in Hong Kong, MIGAC Crystal Crafts has grown into a leading manufacturer of premium crystal candle holders and decorative crafts. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the elegance and beauty of crystal craftsmanship to customers worldwide.

In 2015, we established Yiwu Bangye Handicraft Factory (Yiwu Bangye Handicraft Factory) as our operating entity to better serve our growing international client base. Located at Chengbei Road L38, Houzhai Street, Yiwu, this strategic move positioned us at the heart of China's trade hub while maintaining our production excellence.

Pujiang - China's Crystal Capital is our home base. Our production facilities span across Pujiang Crystal Industrial Parks – the world's largest crystal manufacturing cluster – where we benefit from access to the finest raw materials and a skilled workforce with generations of crystal crafting expertise. This strategic location enables us to maintain exceptional quality while keeping costs competitive.

We can produce any crystal products manufactured in Pujiang County. With the complete industry chain and 15+ years of deep involvement in Pujiang's crystal industry, we have the capability, technology, and expertise to manufacture all types of crystal products that Pujiang is known for.

Over the past 15+ years, we've invested heavily in advanced production technology, quality control systems, and talent development. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of 182+ international clients across 50+ countries and regions.

Today, we produce over 5,000,000 pieces annually, ranging from elegant candle holders to stunning decorative pieces. Each product reflects our dedication to craftsmanship, innovation, and customer satisfaction. With our strong manufacturing capabilities in Pujiang's crystal industrial parks, we offer expert customization services and efficient production for orders of all sizes.
